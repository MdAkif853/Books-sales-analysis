# 📚 Books Sales Analysis - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Books Sales dataset to uncover trends in pricing, ratings, author performance, and sales patterns.

The objective is to identify key business insights that can support strategic decisions in publishing and marketing.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Libraries Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

✔ Distribution of Publishing Year  
✔ Genre-wise Book Distribution  
✔ Rating Count Analysis by Genre  
✔ Sales Price vs Units Sold Correlation  
✔ Average Rating vs Rating Count Analysis  
✔ Total Gross Sales by Author  
✔ Units Sold by Author Rating  
✔ Yearly Sales Trend Analysis  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Modern books (post-2010) dominate the dataset.
- Fiction and Fantasy genres have the highest representation.
- Slight negative correlation observed between price and units sold.
- Top authors contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
- Higher-rated authors tend to generate stronger sales.
- Sales show consistent upward growth over time.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

- Optimize pricing strategy to maximize volume sales.
- Invest marketing efforts in high-performing genres.
- Promote high-rated authors to boost engagement.
- Leverage seasonal trends for sales campaigns.
